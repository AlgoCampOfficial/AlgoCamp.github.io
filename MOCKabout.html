Algo Camp is a project created by Vea Therese Cruz and Reeya Monique Gloriani. The website is a non-profit project that aims to propagate knowledge and training to aspiring programmers. Thus, Algo Camp serves its purpose to those aspiring programmers whilst providing a visual and interactive experience for the users. Furthermore, the creators hope that they can give something back for the community and continue it as much as they can.
